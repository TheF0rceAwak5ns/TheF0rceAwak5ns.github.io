#### unshadow & john
```bash
echo 'root:x:0:0:root:/root:/bin/bash' >> passwd.txt

echo 'root:hash' >> shadow.txt

unshadow passwd.txt shadow.txt > unshadow.txt
```

It's time to crack it!
```bash
john --wordlist=/usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt unshadow.txt
```
